Dr. Nahla Wadie-Salem

- Graduate of the University of Detroit Dental School; Class of 1994.
- Worked thereafter as a clinical instructor until 1996.
- She opened her first private practice in Farmington Hills, on 13 Mile Rd.
- In 2009, she moved to her current location, Dental Spot on Northwestern Hwy.

Dr. Wadie believes strongly in community service. From 1998-2008, she was part of the Oakland County Assistance Program, which offers free service to the underserved members of the community. She also volunteers occasionally at the St. Vincent de Paul Dental Clinic.

Currently, Dr. Wadie works at her private practice 3 days a week, and she is a part-time clinical instructor at the University of Detroit Dental School.
